Dal Makhani

Ingredients

  • Whole black lentils (sabut urad dal) - 1 cup
  • Red kidney beans (rajma) - 1/4 cup
  • Water - 3 cups
  • Butter - 3 tbsp
  • Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Asafoetida - 1/4 tsp
  • Onion - 1 large
  • Tomato - 2 large
  • Ginger - 1 inch
  • Garlic - 4 cloves
  • Green chilli - 1
  • Red chilli powder - 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Cumin powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Garam masala - 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Cream - 1/4 cup
  • Kasuri methi - 1 tsp

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the whole black lentils and red kidney beans in water for 8 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain the water and add the lentils and beans to a pressure cooker along with 3 cups of water.
  3. Pressure cook for 6-7 whistles or until the lentils and beans are cooked.
  4. Heat butter and oil in a pan.
  5. Add cumin seeds and asafoetida.
  6. Add chopped onion and saute until golden brown.
  7. Add ginger, garlic, and green chilli and saute for 2 minutes.
  8. Add chopped tomato and cook until soft.
  9. Add red chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, and salt.
  10. Cook until the oil separates.
  11. Add the cooked lentils and beans along with the water to the pan.
  12. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  13. Add cream and kasuri methi.
  14. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  15. Serve hot with rice or naan.
